About Us

The BMS Educational Trust is a well established institution that has founded and currently manages the BMS College of Engineering, BMS College of Law , BMS College of Architecture and the BMS Hostels. The BMSETH hostels are a group of hostels situated next to the Basavanagudi campus sharing an entrance with BMSCE. There are seven hostels for boys and three for girls among the National Hostels. There is also an International hostel along woth several messes. There is also a provision for games, both indoor and outdoor, for the hostelites!
