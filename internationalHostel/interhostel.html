International Hostel (IH) is mainly dedicated for the NRI and foreign students. It comprises of a total of 5 floors with seperate floors dedicated for male and female students respectively. All rooms have twin sharing capacity and there are 125 rooms in total with a capacity of 250 students.It has its own mess to cater the needs of the international students .It is also well equipped with washing machines for laundry. Recreational facilities include a TV lounge, indoor games like carrom Board,Table Tennis.

BMSET Hostels have two central mess within the campus forboys and girls respectively.They can hold 150 and 60 people at a time respectively.Mess is run by memebers from the hostel committee and Mess Prefects who are elected by the students.The menu for the mess is decided by close co-ordination with nutritionists,wardens,and mess Prefects.It caters to the needs of both vegetarians and non- vegetarians alike.It serves 4 times a day for Breakfast,Lunch,Snacks,Dinner.
